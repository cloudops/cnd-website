Cloud Native Day 2019 will be a full day event bringing together a vibrant, local community full of technical enthusiasts. Attendees will learn how they can deepen their engagement with the fast-growing and dedicated Cloud Native landscape.

Topics

The event will discuss a variety of Cloud Native solutions and CNCF member projects. Talks will be highly-technical and will explore use cases, journeys, and demos that help developers use cloud platforms to build great applications.

Who is this event for?

This event is both for beginners interested in starting their Cloud Native journey as well as early adopters who wish to dig deeper into the projects they have been interacting with for years. Following the recent KubeCon+CloudNativeCon in Seattle, this day-long event will capstone the Kubernetes and Cloud Native meetups that have grown and evolved in Eastern Canada over the course of three years.

Program

Talks will be selected by a committee of Cloud Native evangelists who have been active participants in local communities and have years of industry experience. Four of the eight talks selected will be presented by local experts. The single-track event will be bilingual and we will do our best to select equal amounts of English and French content.
