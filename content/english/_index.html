Cloud Native Day is a full day event, bringing together a vibrant, local community of technical enthusiasts. Attendees will learn how they can deepen their engagement with the fast-growing and complex cloud native landscape.

Topics

The event will discuss a variety of cloud native solutions and projects. Talks will be highly technical and will explore use cases, tool deep dives, and demos.

Who is this event for?

Cloud Native Day is for beginners interested in starting their cloud native journey, as well as early adopters who wish to dig deeper into the projects they have been using.

Thank you! It was great seeing Eastern Canada's communities at Montreal's very first Cloud Native Day on June 11th. Did you miss the event? Videos of all the talks are available here for viewing. Check out a teaser of the event below.

Program

Talks will be selected by a committee of Cloud Native evangelists who have been active participants in local communities and have years of industry experience. Four of the eight talks selected will be presented by local experts. The single-track event will be bilingual and we will do our best to select equal amounts of English and French content.
